Clearing No-Transmit Zones

You can clear all no-transmit zones from the system’s memory. Unlike disabling zones, clearing zones permanently deletes them.

   CAUTION

Clearing no-transmit zones allows the antenna to transmit in any direction without restriction. Make sure everyone stays the minimum safe distance away from the antenna while it is in use (see RF Radiation Hazard Area).

NOTE: This procedure explains how to clear no-transmit zones using the TracPhone V11-IP web interface. If you prefer, you may use the front panel LCD menu instead (go to Diagnostic Tools > Factory Reset).

To clear the no-transmit zones, follow these steps:

  1. At the TracPhone V11-IP web interface, click the Settings tab. Then click No-Transmit Zones.

  2. Click Edit.

  3. If the Login window appears, log in with the Administrator password.

  4. In No-Transmit Zone Ranges, click Clear Zones.

  5. At the confirmation message, click Clear Zones.
