Entering the Vessel Name

The vessel name is displayed on the Home page of the TracPhone V11-IP web interface. It also appears in all technical support communications.

To enter your vessel’s name in the TracPhone V11-IP web interface, follow these steps:

  1. At the TracPhone V11-IP web interface, click the Settings tab. Then click Account.

  2. In Vessel Name, click Edit.

  3. If the Login window appears, log in with the Administrator password.

  4. Enter your vessel’s name.

  5. Click Save Settings.
