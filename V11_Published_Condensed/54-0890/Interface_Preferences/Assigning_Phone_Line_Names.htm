Assigning Phone Line Names

The phone line names are displayed on the Home page of the TracPhone V11-IP web interface. They also appear in all technical support communications. For example, you might assign the name “Bridge” to Line 1 and “Crew Quarters” to Line 2.

To assign a name to each phone line, follow these steps:

  1. At the TracPhone V11-IP web interface, click the Settings tab. Then click Other.

  2. In Phone Line Names, click Edit.

  3. If the Login window appears, log in with the Administrator password.

  4. Enter names for Lines 1 and 2.

  5. Click Save.
