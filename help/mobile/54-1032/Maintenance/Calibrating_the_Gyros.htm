Calibrating the Gyros

The antenna’s gyros continuously measure your rate of motion and send this data to the antenna’s motor control circuitry to keep the antenna pointed at the satellite. At the factory, each gyro is precisely calibrated to work with the antenna’s main board. Therefore, if you ever have a gyro or main board replaced, you will need to recalibrate.

Important!

Calibrate the gyros only if directed by KVH Technical Support, and only while the vessel is stationary in calm seas.

To calibrate the gyros, follow these steps:

  1. At the Support page of the web interface, select Command Line.

  2. Enter =CALNEWGYRO in the text box. Then click Send.

  3. Verify that the gyros pass. If a gyro fails, recalibrate. Once the gyros are calibrated, the antenna restarts.
