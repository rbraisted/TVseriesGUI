Selecting a Satellite Group

The TV-Hub comes preloaded with some of the most popular groups of satellites. To select one of these preset groups for tracking, or to select a custom group that you created yourself, follow these steps:

  1. On the Satellites page of the web interface, set Satellite Mode to Group.

  2. Choose a satellite group from the list. (This list will vary depending on your system’s configuration.) Preset groups are listed below.

    Service

    Preset Satellite Groups (A–B–C–D)

    Linear

    Europe1: Hotbird – Astra1 – Astra2S – Astra3

    Europe 2: Astra3 – Astra1 – Hotbird – Astra2S

    Scandinavia: Astra4 – Thor – Hotbird – Astra1

    DIRECTV U.S.

    101W – 119W

    DISH Network

    Western Arc: 110W – 119W – 129W
    Eastern Arc: 61W – 72W – 77W

    Legacy Arc: 61W – 110W – 119W
    Dual Arc (DISH 500): 110W – 119W

    Bell TV

    91W – 82W

    Tri-Americas (DIRECTV U.S. + Latin America)

    NameTBD: 101W – 119W – 95W
    NameTBD: 101W – 95W

NOTE: If you need to track a different set of satellites, create a custom group. See Creating a New Satellite Group for details.
