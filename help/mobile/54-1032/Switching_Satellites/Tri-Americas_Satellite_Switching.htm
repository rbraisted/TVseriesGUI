Tri-Americas Satellite Switching

If your antenna is equipped with a Tri-Americas LNB, you don’t need to swap the LNB inside the antenna when you switch between DIRECTV U.S. and DIRECTV Latin America services. The LNB can receive satellite signals from either service.

However, when you switch between a DIRECTV U.S. satellite (101W or 119W) and the DIRECTV Latin America satellite (95W), you need to set the switch on the 22KHz tone generator to the correct position for the selected service (refer to the Installation Guide for wiring details):

Service

Satellite

22KHz Switch

DIRECTV U.S.

101W or 119W

OFF

DIRECTV Latin America

95W

ON

22KHz Tone Generator

 

Automatic switching is possible between the 101W and 119W satellites, as explained in Automatic Satellite Switching – DIRECTV. To switch between a DIRECTV U.S. satellite and a DIRECTV Latin America satellite, you need to manually switch using the web interface (see Manual Satellite Switching).
