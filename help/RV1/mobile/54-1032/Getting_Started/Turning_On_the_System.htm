Turning On the System

To turn on your TracVision system, follow these steps:

  1. Make sure the antenna has a clear, unobstructed view of the sky.

  2. At the rear panel of the TV-Hub, set the power switch to the On (|) position. The TV-Hub supplies power to the antenna.

    3. Power Switch (TV-Hub A shown)

     

  3. Wait a few minutes for system startup.

  4. Once the antenna finds the selected satellite, all three status lights on the TV-Hub should be lit green. If any lights are not lit green, refer to TV-Hub Status Indicators.
